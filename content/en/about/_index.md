I'm Yiyao (Jim) Wan, a Software Engineer at Meta based in New York. I work on the MTIA team, building infrastructure to ensure numerics correctness of Meta's Ads models on custom AI accelerators.

My work spans simulation frameworks, data-driven test generation, kernel-level debugging tooling, and LLM-powered agents — all aimed at making AI hardware development faster and more reliable.

Before Meta, I worked at Google, Bloomberg, Walmart, and Aven across areas like distributed systems, query infrastructure, fraud detection, and e-commerce cloud platforms.

I hold an MS in Computer Science from UC San Diego and a BS in Computer Science from UC Santa Barbara. I also won First Place at the Microsoft Azure Responsible AI Hackathon with EasyMed.

**Languages:** Python, C++, TypeScript, Go

**Frameworks:** PyTorch, Kubernetes, Node.js
