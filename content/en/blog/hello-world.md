## Hi, I'm Jim

After work, you'll probably find me listening to Japanese City Pop, playing League of Legends, or hanging out with my wife — city walks, tennis, cooking, and more.

## Why This Site?

The idea of building a personal website has been on my mind for a long time, but I never got around to it. Then I saw my good friend Yi Zhang (张熠) build a website reflecting on his career path at [yizhang7210.com](https://yizhang7210.com/), and it inspired me to finally make it happen — with a little help from AI.

Here's what I wanted:

- **A home base.** Somewhere that isn't LinkedIn or Twitter — a space I fully control.
- **A place to write.** I have thoughts that don't fit into a tweet or a code review comment. Some are about technology, some about life, some are just 碎碎念 (random musings).
- **A vibe.** I wanted something that feels warm and calm. Hence the cream-colored design and the lofi BGM player in the corner — hit that music note button if you haven't already.

## The Stack

For the curious, this site is built with:

- **Hugo** — a fast static site generator with great multilingual support
- **Custom theme** — hand-crafted glassmorphism design with warm cream tones
- **SCSS** via Hugo Pipes — zero Node.js dependencies
- **GitHub Pages** — free hosting, deployed via GitHub Actions
- **Bilingual** — full English and Chinese support, because I think in both

The entire site builds in under 200ms. No JavaScript frameworks, no build tools beyond Hugo itself.

## What's Next?

No fixed themes here. This blog is a mix of whatever's on my mind — small joys at work, career reflections, life musings, things I cooked, places I walked, games I played. Some posts might be technical, some might just be a few sentences. That's the point of 碎碎念.

Thanks for stopping by.
